Adding your own images

Ok so now you know how to add images, but what if you want to add your own images from files? Here's how you can do that (disclaimer: you need to use an outside site)

Step 1: Add to files

The first step is to make sure that your image is on files. You can do this by clicking an image off of the web and clicking the "add to files" button. If the image is on your phone you can email it to yourself, and from your laptop, download the image to files. Also remember to name it something you won't forget (so not a random mix of letters and numbers).

Step 2: Use imagekit

1. Open this link: Image Kit Link

2. Once you are in imagekit, click the "upload here" button. This will redirect you to files, either click the file you saved earlier, or the file you already wanted to use.

2. The image will appear like this on the site. Click the grey three lines, and click "Copy direct CDN URL". The image is now copied to your clipboard.

Step 3: Add image

You should already know how to add images to your site, but here's a quick recap. Type the following into your code.

< img

src = "yourlink.com"

/ >

Good job, you're done!
